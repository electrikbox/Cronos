How to pause a cron

What does it mean to pause a cron?

A paused cron won't run until you reactivate it. It's still there for you if you need it later

To pause a cron, click on the pause icon on the right. The icon will then change to a play button, and your cron row will turn grey.

When you're ready to reactivate your cron, simply click on this play button
