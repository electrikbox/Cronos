How to fill in the OPEN command field

As a reminder, the OPEN command will open the url of your choice in a new tab of your default browser at the defined time.

This command presents you with a url field, in which you must enter the url of the site you wish to open.


Please note that http:// or https:// must be entered for the url to be correctly taken into account.


TIPS : Open the website you want and copy the url directly from your browser's URL field !
