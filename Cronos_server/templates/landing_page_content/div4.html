About

Our project grew out of experience gained during an internship, when the idea of simplifying the management of scheduled tasks on a computer system was raised. Initially envisioned as a cron management tool for internal use, we quickly realized the potential of making this functionality accessible to a wider audience

The aim was to create an intuitive online service that would enable anyone to schedule automated tasks on their computer, without the need for in-depth technical knowledge

Our team set about designing and developing this platform with the mission of simplifying IT processes for everyone, while guaranteeing a fluid and secure user experience.

The result is a reliable, user-friendly service that enables everyone to maximize the efficiency of their computer use, whatever their level of experience and technical skills
